About

Festember, a fest to remember, is the annual cultural extravaganza of NIT Trichy organized in month of September. Since its inception in 1975, it has established itself as the best in South India winning the state best cultural festival award. A 4-day run course of Festember hosts more than 50 breathtaking events and electrifying pro-show performances by artists of international fame.
